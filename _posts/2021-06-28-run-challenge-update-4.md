### Recovery Runs
I had two recovery runs spread out, when it came around to my second recovery run I experienced problems.  I actually felt great after my 45 minute recovery run, I didn't think I went too fast or anything.  However a few hours later, I started feeling pinching pain in my achilles while in flexion.  Being so worried that it was very difficult for me to walk on my right foot, I searched remedies and reasons behind this type of injury.  I iced the area 2 more times that night before I went to bed.  I woke up with a milder pain, but luckily with a rest day planned I did not have to worry about running and could focus on the recovery, icing throughout.  The following day it felt great actually, which was quite surprising I'd say about 90% recovered at best.  Instead of jumping in to the routine I thought i'd create another recovery day so that I don't jump straight into the routine (speed/long run).  The run itself was quite slow which is fine by me as long as I didn't aggravate the injury, I made sure this time to be extra careful by doing warmups (calf raises, lunges) and cooldown stretches afterwards.
