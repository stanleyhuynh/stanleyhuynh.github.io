# Running Challenge Update 6 - Results
I am super thankful to have a friend with me to help pace me for this run.  I am no where close to a good runner so that was such an amazing boost to help me hit my target.

### 10 KM Warmup
For this I did a light jog for about a minute, then did some light interval sprints (Rated Perceived Exertion(RPE) of 8/10) this was to help me get my breathing in check before the actual run.  I did very light stretching afterwards to prepare and off I went for the final run.

### Final 10 KM
This run started out quite well, I'd say at about 3km point I was starting to feel like I was lagging behind and had to increase my energy exerted to keep the 5:30 km pace.  At around the 5km point it was hard for me to keep up, I had to keep my attention of from the tiredness of my legs and just focus on following behind.

I kept it going until around the 8 KM mark when I started feeling minor cramps.  I was supposed to start speeding up as originally planned to squeeze out the last bit of energy from me so that I'd have no regrets that I didn't push myself enough.

At the final km I really started to feel the tiredness, I was already breathing heavily at around 5km mark but this was where I pushed myself.  The average pace for each km was just above 5:30 but my final km was done at 5:03, I just sprinted at the end looking at my watch waiting for the number to hit 10 KM and once I reached it I immediately went to the floor from exhaustion.

And the moment of truth!  The stats are below, but essentially from the Nike Run Club (NRC) app I finished at 55:09 which is just above my intending sub 55 minute 10km.

![Final Run Stats](/assets/img/2021-07-07-final-run-stats.png)

Although this was the results on the NRC app, I think I completed the challenge.  My friend tracked the same run and got the sub 55 minute time, looking at the route details it looks like there was one split at my 5km that spiked to 5:48/km but this looked like an error in catching my turn around.  I specifically recalled that when we turned around we did not stop (NRC recorded a drop to 14:15/km) which should not add +15 seconds to that split.

![Final Run Route Details](/assets/img/2021-07-07-final-run-route-details.png)

### Overall I think this challenge was a complete success, I hit a PR of a 55 minute 10 KM and I look forward to reaching new running milestones in the future.  But for now I will rest up and prepare for my next challenge.
