This is officially my first blog post and a start to trying to respark my passion for running. Of all things this will aim to motivate myself, as well as to give realistic expectations of what preparing for a 10 km entails.  I've never really followed any running plans before.

# 4 Week - 10 km Running Challenge
### What is this challenge?
Essentially my goal is to follow a 4 week (30 day) training plan to build up to a 10 km (6.2 miles) run.  

### Why am I doing this?
Ideally this would be for some sort of 10 km running event, but due to the circumstances in the world or Canada where I live for that matter, these events are held virtually so I will complete this challenge on my schedule.

I hope this challenge will help me in the following: learn training routines, types of running, improve my running form, better physical conditioning (lacking during the pandemic), and to hopefully achieve a sub-55 minute 10 km time.  For context, my personal best was just under an hour and honestly I would be very happy with this but lets push ourselves!

### What training plan am I doing?
The training plan I am doing is simply following the Nike Run Club - 10 km training plan.  This plan is normally an 8-week routine, but due to the challenge and my ability to stick to routines for so long I will cut it short to 4 weeks which is the minimal recommended preparation time.
