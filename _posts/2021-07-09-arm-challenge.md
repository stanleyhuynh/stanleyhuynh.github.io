# 30 day Arm Challenge
### What is this challenge?
Here my goal is to get bigger arms my goal to gain an inch in my arms within the time frame.

Note I have not worked out at a gym over a year, and haven't strength trained at all for over half a year.  So I expect beginner gains to help me hit my goal currently my arm is 12 3/4 inches before any workout.

I will not be gaining weight for this challenge and I am hoping I can stay within +/- 2 pounds of my starting weight of 150.  I am hoping final measurement will be before a workout so preferably morning after a workout.

### What will I be doing?
My plan for this challenge is to alternate workouts between bicep and tricep, trying to build as much muscle for these two muscles.  

Each of the workouts i'll make sure to wear out all the muscles for the bicep (short head, long head, brachialis) and for the tricep (lateral, long, medial head).
