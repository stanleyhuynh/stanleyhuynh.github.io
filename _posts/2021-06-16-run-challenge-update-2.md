My update for the end of my first week, the the two runs that stood out to me were quite tough for me physically and mentally.

# Running Challenge Update 2
### Tempo Run (Speed)
This type of run was quite different for me, it reminded me of the high intensity interval training (HIIT) that I have tried years ago.  I have not pushed myself to exert myself at RPE of 10 in a very long time and I felt the effects of being a little winded afterwards.  I had to take longer breaks in between to handle my discomfort but hope I can cut down my breaks in the future.

I think this could be a great addition to my training as it will get me used to pushing my speed and used to being uncomfortable.

### Long Run (10 km)
I walked in to this run thinking I could not do it, I have only done a 10 km run less than a handful of times.  My thought was that I would take it slow which I did going at around a 7 min/km pace.  This run in particular felt more of a mental challenge as I knew I had to do the time, I kept looking back at my watch as I pushed 10, 20, 30 minutes and finally I just went on auto pilot until 10 km.  This was a longer, slower run than what I want in my end goal for this challenge but I know it's necessary to get me to a sub-55 minute 10 km.

Post run I definitely felt the impact, I had to replenish with food and my legs felt sore.  Currently this post is after a day of recovery and I can't believe that my legs aren't more sore, I will continue the plan starting week 3 of the Nike 10 km plan.

### End of my first week, start of new week and I will try to keep going strong.
