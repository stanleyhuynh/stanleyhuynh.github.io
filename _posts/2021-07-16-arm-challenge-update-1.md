# Arm Challenge Update 1
### Bicep workout
My workout here is based off Athlean-X youtube channel: https://youtu.be/gozU3CUIizs. I find it quite informative in terms of providing detailed explanations of function, how to work on the muscle group.  The day after I started feeling the delayed onset muscle soreness (DOMS) quite badly but I do hope it will get better over time as I get back into the rhythm.

### Tricep workout
Workout for this muscle group is also based off this youtube channel at: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hctR7BCmvVs.  Quite simpler in terms of workout, no super sets and the volume is much less than the bicep workout.  However I still think this is quite efficient I just need to work out enough to get the effects to promote muscle growth.  Again DOMS was in effect the day after.

### First week recap
One bad thing that I've been doing is not keeping track of the weights of each exercise, because it would help with incorporating progressive overload to increase intensity over the one month time.  However this might be hard since this challenge is essentially alternating between exercising my bicep and tricep and wearing out one muscle will most likely effect the performance of me doing other exercises.

In terms of growth my arms are at about 13 1/4 inch, a day after a workout.  Measurements will fluctuate if I workout the day of, so I need to figure out how I determine the success of this challenge.  An idea might be to see how the size of my arm decreases over the week after my last workout.
