### First Run
First run for this challenge was quite rough, went at a "slower" pace that I am used to running which I believe is correct for this recovery day.  Although I went at a pace that was slower, I noticed that my endurance was very poor because I had a 5 minute break near the midway point. Takeaway for my next recovery run is to go even slower, my goal would be to not stop at all next time.

![Run Stats](/assets/img/2021-06-09-run-stats.PNG)
### Second Run
For my second run, this was a new concept for me as I have never done a tempo run and to be honest I had no idea what I was doing.  Based off the descriptions from the Nike training plan, I failed the criteria of covering the same distance on my way back. The first interval I was going at roughly a 6 min/km pace and for the second interval I averaged around 6.5 min/km pace.

This is revealing to me that I am in worse condition than I was years back.  At this rate I would not be able to complete the 10 km run in under an hour.
